How to get your Twitter archive:
  1. Go to X.com → Settings → Your account → Download an archive of your data
  2. Request the archive and wait for the email (up to 24 hours)
  3. Download the .zip file — no need to unzip it
  4. Drag the .zip directly below, or click to select it
You can also drop individual .js files from an already-unzipped archive.
