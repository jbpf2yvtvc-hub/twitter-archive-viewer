How to get your Twitter archive:
  1. Go to X.com → Settings → Your account → Download an archive of your data
  2. Request the archive and wait for the email (can take up to 24 hours)
  3. Download and unzip the archive file
  4. Drag the entire unzipped folder below, or select individual .js files
